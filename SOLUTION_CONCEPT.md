# Intelligent Case Portal (IACP) - Solution Concept & User Guide

## 1. Executive Summary
The **Intelligent Case Portal (IACP)** is a next-generation adjudicatory ecosystem designed to modernize the Department of Labor (DOL) Office of Administrative Law Judges (OALJ) and Appellate Boards (BRB, ARB, ECAB). By unifying the external **Unified Filing Service (UFS)** and the internal **Intelligent Case Portal (IACP)**, the system eliminates traditional silos, leverages Generative AI for real-time validation, and provides a secure, collaborative environment for judicial decision-making.

---

## 2. Current State & Pain Points
Based on current DOL adjudicatory requirements, the following pain points are addressed by this solution:
*   **Manual Intake Burden**: Clerks spend excessive time manually verifying SSNs, signatures, and document completeness.
*   **Fragmented Systems**: External filers (EFS) and internal adjudicators (CTS/AMS) operate in disconnected environments, leading to data latency and manual re-entry.
*   **Lack of Collaborative Drafting**: Judges and Law Clerks often rely on external word processors and email for decision drafting, creating security risks and version control issues.
*   **Opaque Appellate Records**: Moving a case from OALJ to the Boards often involves manual "record preparation" which delays the appellate process.
*   **High Barrier to Entry**: New users and stakeholders struggle to understand the complex end-to-end legal lifecycle.

---

## 3. The Solution: Intelligent Case Portal (IACP)
IACP is built on a **Unified Adjudicatory Architecture** that treats the entire case lifecycle as a single digital thread.

### 3.1 Logical Architecture
*   **Frontend (React/Tailwind)**: A high-performance, responsive UI that adapts to the user's role (Attorney, Claimant, Clerk, Judge).
*   **AI Service Layer (Gemini API)**: Integrated GenAI that performs OCR, PII detection, and legal summarization in real-time.
*   **Secure Integration Layer**: Designed to sit atop existing EFS/CTS/AMS databases, utilizing current technologies while providing a modern interface.
*   **Chambers Workspace**: A "Chinese Wall" protected environment for internal-only research and drafting.

---

## 4. Key Features

### 4.1 AI-Powered Intake (UFS)
*   **Real-time Validation**: AI scans uploaded documents (e.g., Claim Forms) for mandatory fields like SSN, Date of Injury, and Signatures *before* the user clicks submit.
*   **PII Detection**: Automatically flags unredacted sensitive information to ensure compliance with privacy regulations.
*   **Automated Categorization**: AI identifies the program area (BLA, LHC, FECA) based on document content.

### 4.2 High-Speed Internal Docketing
*   **Priority Queue**: AI pre-sorts filings based on urgency and completeness.
*   **One-Click Docketing**: Clerks can verify AI findings and assign a docket number in seconds.
*   **Return-to-Filer Workflow**: Automated deficiency notices generated by AI for incomplete filings.

### 4.3 Collaborative Chambers Workspace
*   **Private Drafting**: A secure editor for Judges and Law Clerks to collaborate on decisions.
*   **Redline Mode**: Real-time tracking of edits and internal-only comments.
*   **Integrated Research**: Bench memos and exhibit flags linked directly to the digital record.

### 4.4 Interactive Walkthrough Module
*   **Stakeholder Demos**: A built-in simulation engine that allows stakeholders to "play" through the case lifecycle.
*   **Role-Based Perspectives**: Switch between Attorney, Clerk, and Judge views to understand the full impact of the system.

## 5. User Guide

### 5.1 For External Filers (Attorneys/Claimants)
1.  **Login**: Access the UFS Portal via Login.gov.
2.  **New Filing**: Select "New Case Filing" or "New Motion".
3.  **Upload**: Drag and drop your legal documents.
4.  **AI Check**: Review the "AI Intake Analysis" panel. Fix any flagged errors (e.g., missing signature).
5.  **Submit**: Receive your instant Intake ID and tracking confirmation.

### 5.2 For Internal Clerks
1.  **Intake Queue**: Monitor the "Priority Intake Queue" for new submissions.
2.  **Verify**: Review the AI-extracted metadata and verification status.
3.  **Action**: Click "Assign Docket #" to create the case or "Return to Filer" if deficiencies are found.

### 5.3 For Judges & Law Clerks
1.  **My Docket**: View active cases sorted by statutory deadlines.
2.  **Chambers**: Open the "Chambers Workspace" for a specific case.
3.  **Collaborate**: Use the Bench Memo and Exhibit Flags to organize research.
4.  **Draft**: Prepare the decision in the secure editor, review redlines from staff, and electronically sign to release to the docket.

---

## 6. Modernization Pitch: Why IACP?

### 6.1 Efficiency Gains
By moving validation to the "edge" (the filer's browser), IACP reduces the clerk's manual verification workload by an estimated **80-90%**.

### 6.2 Data Integrity
Direct integration between UFS and IACP ensures that the data entered by the filer is the same data used by the Judge, eliminating transcription errors.

### 6.3 Enhanced Security
The Chambers Workspace provides a dedicated, encrypted environment for pre-decisional work, ensuring that internal deliberations never leak to the public record.

### 6.4 Scalability
The architecture is designed to support all DOL adjudicatory program areas (Black Lung, Longshore, FECA, SOX, etc.) within a single, unified framework.

By combining the power of **Generative AI** with a **Unified User Experience**, IACP delivers a faster, more accurate, and more transparent adjudicatory process for all Americans.
